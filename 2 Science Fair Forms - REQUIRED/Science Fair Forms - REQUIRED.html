Science Fair Forms

The following forms need to be completed BEFORE you start your Science Fair experiment:

  • Checklist for Adult Sponsor
  • Student Checklist & Research Plan Instructions (Instructions Only)
  • Student Checklist
  • Approval Form

Be sure to submit ALL three forms (either online or in class)!

Basic Research Paperwork for All Students

Students must complete ISEF Forms 1, 1A, 1B, a Research Plan, and the NCSEF Abstract for their projects. Additional documentation may be required for projects involving humans, animals, hazardous materials, or continuation projects.

Students must follow the ISEF Rules and Regulations for their research.

Important Notices

  • At the start of the project, an Institutional Review Board (IRB) or Scientific Review Committee (SRC), typically your teacher, must review and approve most projects involving human participants, vertebrate animals, or potentially hazardous biological agents.
  • Note: If a project involves the testing of a student-designed invention, prototype, or concept by a human, an IRB review and approval may be required before experimentation. See Human Participants Rules for details.
  • Form Submission: All forms must be filled out and signed before any research begins. (Forms 1C, 5B, 7, and the Abstract are completed after the research.) Ensure that the dates of signatures reflect when approval or consent was given. Use the MM/DD/YY format for all dates.
