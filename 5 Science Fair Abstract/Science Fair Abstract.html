Experiment must be completed, or preliminary data gathered before the abstract can be written!

Abstract Guidelines

Abstract: Write a paragraph no more than 250 words.

Must include a few sentences for each area below:

  • Purpose of the experiment
  • Procedure (summarize; don't list)
  • Data (summarize data; no data charts)
  • Conclusions (What I learned…)
  • Analysis (If I did this project again…)

Project Categories

Students may submit projects in the following categories:

  • Biological Science A – Animal science, medicine and health science, microbiology
  • Biological Science B – Cellular and molecular biology, biochemistry, behavioral and social sciences, plant sciences
  • Chemistry – Composition, structure, properties, and reactions of matter
  • Earth Environmental Science – Geology, oceanography, meteorology, climatology, pollution studies
  • Physics – Matter, energy, and interactions between them
  • Math, Statistics, Data Science
  • Technology/Engineering – Applying scientific principles to practical uses
